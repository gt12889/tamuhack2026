# Elder Strolls - System Design & User Experience Workflow

## Executive Summary

Elder Strolls is a voice-first travel assistance platform for elderly American Airlines passengers. It combines conversational AI, real-time location tracking, and remote caregiver assistance to help seniors navigate flight changes, disruptions, and airport wayfinding.

**Phone:** +1 (877) 211-0332
